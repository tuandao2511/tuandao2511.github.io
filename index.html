you know that some phones (like Samsung or Sony for example) have a PowerSaving Mode that might stop alarms from working? Not to miss your alarms, turn off Power Saving mode or add Smart Alarm into your whitelist. Also, if you use third-party memory cleaners, make sure they do not unload the application from memory

Whitelist

Here we have the instructions to add the appinto whitelist for specific manufactures

  1. Go to Settings -> Power Saving
  2. See if you can add Smart Alarm to the protected whitelist. Also, if your device has a memory manager (tap a right square button) you need to lock the app. Just click on the lock icon. If you do not have such settings, just ignore it.
